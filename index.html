FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know. Can't find your answer? Just give us a shout.

Do you offer free consultations?
Yes — completely free. We come to your home or business, take measurements, look at the space, and talk through what you're after. No pressure, no sales pitch.
Can I choose the materials, colours, and finishes?
100%. Everything is bespoke — you pick the timber, finish, colour, handles, hinges, all of it. We'll guide you through the options and find the right combination for your space and budget.
Do you work outside of Cork?
We're Cork-based and do most of our work in Cork city and county, but we're happy to travel for the right project. Get in touch and we'll let you know.
Are you fully insured?
Yes — fully qualified and insured. Total peace of mind that your home and the work are covered throughout the entire job.
What's included in the quote?
Everything — design, materials, build, delivery, and installation. We give you a fixed price before we start. What we quote is what you pay. No surprises.
Do you organise all other trades for projects?
Yes — we look after the project from start to finish, including all necessary trades and materials for that specific project.
